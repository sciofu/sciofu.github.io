Project Description

The Problem

The specific problem right now is the unavailability of one source of truth for rescued animals in need of a home. Aditionally, there is not an easy method to look for good services or reviews about them when talking about pet owners.

Value Proposition

A place where all pet services coexist in a proactive environment driven by pet owners. A platform where pet sitters and veterinarians can offer their services and be reviewed by the community.

Customer Segment

Our customer base will consist of prospective pet adopters looking to connect with the pet a little more personally than a random forum announcement allows.

Competition

There is no cohesive platform for pet caring in Romania right now so our competition is quite disparate: OLX, social media adoption groups and individual sites (such as animalutul.ro, adoptii-animale-hope.org and others). Our main advantage is that we wil provide dedicated adoption place to centralize all of the posts on those platforms.

Key Metrics

As our main goal is to help our users help pets in need, we believe our main metric needs to be the adoption ratio (number of adoptions / number of users; since we aim for every user that uses our services to land an adoption, our target is for this metric to be as close to 1 as possible).

Cost Structure

For the initial deployment the fixed costs are represented by the domain name and marketing. For varibale costs we have the cloud services necessary for the platform that will scale up with the number of users.

Revenue Streams

We target a revenue driven by ads, but we also consider doing partnerships with trusted pet shops and vets that will be promoted in the app.

Validation

How we identified the problem

We have a few pet owners in our team, as well as a few members who are considering it.

We came to the conclusion that the adoption process is quite arduous due to the multitude of platforms that different adoption centers and people with stray pets use (some use Instagram, some use OLX, some use blogs, etc.)

What we thought is a solution for the problem

We have decided that a dedicated platform for pet adoption and ownership is something that would greatly improve this sometimes daunting process.

We have also considered that a platform offering adoption alternatives could also help convince users to give this option a try.

Our plan for customer discovery

We decided to conduct an online survey to give us insight into how the genereal process of adoption works, how people use the current market and what are the problems and needs of pet owners.

The process

We made a survey using Google Forms that we distributed using social networks and direct contact with a few friends. This way we made sure that it gets to socially active persons, the kind of users that we are targeting in the beginning at least.

We managed to reach 98 persons, the majority, 89, being in the 20-25 age group.

The questions varried from "how would you describe the adoption process?" to "what services do you use or would like to have in order to accomodate your pet?".

How these insights have affected our product

We discovered that pet adoption and ownership is influenced by friends and acquaitances. Therefore our app needs to be socially driven network where you cand see pets from animal shelters, stray pets from friends, connect with friends and see from where they adopted.

An inside blog with topics such as nutrition, cosmetics and competitions should also be present, as those are of interest to owners.

Profiles with reviews for vets, tamers and pet hotels, with a dedicated map to see those available nearby because we discovered that people value a lot places close to their home.

Are we ready for Customer Validation

We have our features stated above so we are ready to continue with the design of the app and how we will have them integrated.

Interviews

For this part of the process we decided to interview some pet owners, as it turned out they are going to be the first users of our application. We asked them the same set of core questions, but we also adapted based on their answers. Some of the core questions are:

  • Hi! Tell us something about yourself.
  • What pets do you own?
  • How did you get them and why? Tell us about the whole process
  • What difficulties did you encounter durint this process?
  • How did you find the services that you are using for your pet?
  • What are you considering when evaluating these services?
  • Are you considering getting another pet?
  • How often do you interact with other pet owners? Are you usually asking them for help? How do you find these connections?
Interview Scripts

Below are the transcripts of the 3 most relevant discussions we've had.

Person 1

Salut, zi-ne ceva despre tine! -

- Hello! Imi plac cainii de mor, am avut pana in prezent peste 10 catei. Acum am împreună cu prietenul meu un Golden Retriever de 2 ani pe nume Beni

Cum ai ajuns sa intri in posesia lui Beni si de ce? -

- Prietenul meu isi dorea de foarte mult timp un caine, iar eu i-am propus sa adoptam. In vremea aceea el statea singur si simtea nevoia sa mai aiba pe cineva in casa, in cazul asta o codita fericita. Alt motiv a fost si responsabilizarea.

Cool, cum a decurs procesul de adoptie? -

- Pe Beni l-am luat de pe olx. A venit o familie din Ploiesti si ni l-a adus acasa. Gen nu e nici rescued nici bought. Gen initial ne cerusera 50 de lei dar cand l-au vazut pe sebi atat de fericit au refuzat sa ne mai ia banii. Si aia 50 de lei erau asa ca sa fie. Beni avea 3 luni cand l-am luat.

Ce dificultati ati intampinat sau intampinati in cresterea lui Beni? -

- A fost stricat la stomac la o luna dupa ce l-am luat, a trebuit sa ii scoatem un cos intr-o pozitie dubioasa, a alergat o data si s-a lovit la picior, in rest nimic altceva. Prietenul meu l-a dresat si l-a invatat sa nu faca in casa.

Cum v-ati ales serviciile pentru Beni? -

- Vetul il stim de la oamenii cu care ieseam in tarc in cismigiu. Si tot ce inseamna mancare stim de la vet si de la oameni care au catei. Si de pe grupul golden retriever romania mai luam info legate de vet sau mancare jucarii.

Va ganditi sa mai luati un animal de companie in curand? Da/Nu, de ce? -

- In curand nu pentru ca e spațiul foarte mic si trebuie sa ne mutam. Dar daca ar fi spatiul mare am mai lua o pisica sau un catel.

Person 2

Salut, zi-ne ceva despre tine! -

- Hei hei! Mersi și eu. Ma numesc Ana, am 3 animale de companie, un cățel și 2 pisici. Toti sunt maidanezi.

Cum ai ajuns sa intri in posesia fiecarui animal si de ce? -

- Pai îmi plac animalele de companie de când eram mica, mai ales ideea de a cumpăra un animal nu este tocmai potrivita pentru mine și prefer sa adopt sau sa le iau de pe strada. Cățelul l am găsit în Arad, pe lângă un tomberon, una dintre pisici a apărut în fata curții mele și motanul este puiul unei pisici pe care am găsit o pe strada

Ce dificultati intampini in cresterea animalelor? -

- La pisici e ok, in schimb cainii sunt greu de crescut, trebuie sa-i inveti unde sa-si faca nevoile, sa nu muste, sunt ca niste copii mici.

Cum ai ales serviciile pentru animalele tale de companie? -

- Din păcate nu a ținut de mine ci de părinții mei.

Cum stii daca serviciul pe care l-ai ales pentru cainele tau a fost facut bine? -

- La început eram sceptica în alegerea părinților mei, dar după ce am castrat câinele și am văzut ca operația s a vindecat minunat am zis ca a fost o alegere buna.

Te gandesti sa mai iei un animal in curand? Da/Nu, de ce? -

- Da, as vrea sa adopt un animal cu handicap sau un animal bătrân. Mi se pare ca animalele cu handicap sunt speciale și nu am avut. Iar cele bătrâne merita iubire pana în ultimul moment al vieții lor.

Cat de mult interactionezi cu alti stapani de animale? In ce mod interactionati? Ceri vreodata sfaturi/ajutor de la cunostinte cu animale de companie? -

- Destul de des interacționez cu alți stăpâni de animale, când vad câini pe strada nu ma pot abține și vreau sa i mângâi pe toți și de cele mai multe ori ajungem sa vorbim despre asta și încerc sa țin cont de sfaturile lor.

Person 3

Salut, zi-ne ceva despre tine! -

- Am 23 de ani, am terminat limbi straine, sunt profesoara de engleza si coordonator ONG de voluntariat, am un chihuahua de vreo 8 ani si o pisica de cateva luni cu care poate petrec cam 2-3 ore pe zi.

Cum ai ajuns sa intri in posesia fiecarui animal si de ce? -

- Am adoptat catelul de la niste cunostinte care nu mai puteau sa aiba grija de el, iar pisica am gasit-o pe strada.

Ce dificultati intampini in cresterea animalelor? -

- Catelul e predispus la raceli si are si o problema hormonala, deci mergem la veterinar cam o data la 2 saptamani.

Ce servicii mai folosesti in afara de veterinar? -

- Eu nu prea folosesc alte servicii, dar parintii mei au un cocker care are nevoie destul de des sa mearga la tuns.

Cum ai ales serviciile astea? -

- Le-am gasit prin intermediul cunostintelor, insa in ceea ce priveste veterinarul am cautat ceva in apropierea casei in mod deosebit.

Cum stii ca serviciile sunt bune? -

- Am incredere in recomandarile prietenilor, caut si review-uri de pe internet, si imi formez o opinie si din experienta de la fata locului.

Mai vrei sa iti iei inca un animal? -

- Nu mai vreau acum pentru ca stau la 2 camere si sunt deja suficiente animale in casa.

Mai ai interactiuni cu alti stapani de animale? -

- In parcurile de catei stai 20 de minute si ajungi sa vorbesti cu alti stapani despre ce dai de mancare, ce chestii si-au mai invatat cainii, and so on.

Ai cautat vreodata articole de dresaj, tips and tricks, orice in zona asta? -

- Am cautat in special cand am luat pisica, deoarece cainele intrase in depresie. Internetul a avut foarte multe informatii, insa nu au dat roade si a recurs la sfatul unui veterinar pentru a rezolva problema.

Personas

Based on the interview we managed to create tow personas for our users.

Alice
Demographics
  • 23 years old
  • has higher education
  • socially active
Behaviour
  • feels alone and wants a pet for company
  • first priority is finding a vet
Needs
  • services need to be of a good quality, preferably close to home
  • needs guidance/advice in educating the pet
Problems
  • has very particular questions that do not have an easily accessible answer in online articles
Bob
Demographics
  • 25 years old
  • young couple
  • socially active
Behaviour
  • their pets are mostly adopted or rescued
  • enjoy sharing experiences with other pet owners
Needs
  • vets need to be kind and empathetic to the pets
  • needs guidance/advice in educating the pet
Problems
  • would like another pet, but the living space does not allow
Interviews

We discovered the following user stories based on our interviews:

  • I want to adopt a pet
  • I want advice on how to train them
  • I want a good vet close to home
  • I want to know how other pet owners take care of their pets
  • I want somebody to take care of my pet while I am away from home
  • I want to know when my friends are giving animals out for adoption
