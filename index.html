Project Description

The Problem

The specific problem right now is the unavailability of one source of truth for rescued animals in need of a home. Aditionally, there is not an easy method to look for good services or reviews about them when talking about pet owners.

Value Proposition

A place where all pet services coexist in a proactive environment driven by pet owners. A platform where pet sitters and veterinarians can offer their services and be reviewed by the community.

Customer Segment

Our customer base will consist of prospective pet adopters looking to connect with the pet a little more personally than a random forum announcement allows.

Competition

There is no cohesive platform for pet caring in Romania right now so our competition is quite disparate: OLX, social media adoption groups and individual sites (such as animalutul.ro, adoptii-animale-hope.org and others). Our main advantage is that we wil provide dedicated adoption place to centralize all of the posts on those platforms.

Key Metrics

As our main goal is to help our users help pets in need, we believe our main metric needs to be the adoption ratio (number of adoptions / number of users; since we aim for every user that uses our services to land an adoption, our target is for this metric to be as close to 1 as possible).

Cost Structure

For the initial deployment the fixed costs are represented by the domain name and marketing. For varibale costs we have the cloud services necessary for the platform that will scale up with the number of users.

Revenue Streams

We target a revenue driven by ads, but we also consider doing partnerships with trusted pet shops and vets that will be promoted in the app.

Validation

How we identified the problem

We have a few pet owners in our team, as well as a few members who are considering it.

We came to the conclusion that the adoption process is quite arduous due to the multitude of platforms that different adoption centers and people with stray pets use (some use Instagram, some use OLX, some use blogs, etc.)

What we thought is a solution for the problem

We have decided that a dedicated platform for pet adoption and ownership is something that would greatly improve this sometimes daunting process.

We have also considered that a platform offering adoption alternatives could also help convince users to give this option a try.

Our plan for customer discovery

We decided to conduct an online survey to give us insight into how the genereal process of adoption works, how people use the current market and what are the problems and needs of pet owners.

The process

We made a survey using Google Forms that we distributed using social networks and direct contact with a few friends. This way we made sure that it gets to socially active persons, the kind of users that we are targeting in the beginning at least.

We managed to reach 98 persons, the majority, 89, being in the 20-25 age group.

The questions varried from "how would you describe the adoption process?" to "what services do you use or would like to have in order to accomodate your pet?".

How these insights have affected our product

We discovered that pet adoption and ownership is influenced by friends and acquaitances. Therefore our app needs to be socially driven network where you cand see pets from animal shelters, stray pets from friends, connect with friends and see from where they adopted.

An inside blog with topics such as nutrition, cosmetics and competitions should also be present, as those are of interest to owners.

Profiles with reviews for vets, tamers and pet hotels, with a dedicated map to see those available nearby because we discovered that people value a lot places close to their home.

Are we ready for Customer Validation

We have our features stated above so we are ready to continue with the design of the app and how we will have them integrated.
